4. Billing Terms 
Billing Method 

The Service Provider shall invoice the Client on a monthly basis for all services provided during the applicable billing period. 

Service charges will be calculated according to the pricing structure defined in this Agreement and will be based on actual operational usage including: 

Number of flights handled 

Terminal and facility usage hours 

Aircraft landing coordination activities 

Ground operations support services provided 

All services will be documented through operational service reports and activity logs, which may be used by the Client for verification of invoice amounts. 

Invoices shall be issued at the end of each billing period and shall include detailed service descriptions, quantities, unit rates, and total charges. 

 

Billing frequency: Monthly 

Payment terms: Net 30 days 

Currency: USD
